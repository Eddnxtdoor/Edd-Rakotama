About Me

I was born in Kimberly, South Africa, a place that, while not known for its technology, played a role in shaping who I am today. My love for innovation and technology was sparked by two key influences. Growing up, I was captivated by the TV show MacGyver, where the protagonist's ability to create ingenious solutions with limited resources inspired my curiosity about problem-solving and the potential of technology. My father also played a significant role in fostering my interest, as he often encouraged me to explore how things work and taught me the importance of creativity in understanding technology.

With a solid understanding of HTML and some experience in CSS, I’m eager to continue my journey in web development. I look forward to learning JavaScript and Python to create more dynamic, interactive projects that blend creativity with functionality. These skills will enable me to bring my ideas to life in exciting new ways.

Driven by the thrill of turning ideas into reality, I aspire to use my skills to develop applications that make a meaningful impact. I’m committed to growing as a developer and exploring the endless possibilities technology offers to improve lives and solve real-world problems.
